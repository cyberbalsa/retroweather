# Join the Closed Test

Retro Weather is available as a closed test on Google Play. Follow these two steps to get access:

## 1. Join the Google Group

Request membership at the testing group:

https://groups.google.com/u/1/g/retroweather-testing

## 2. Opt in to the test

Once you're a group member, open the testing link to opt in:

https://play.google.com/apps/testing/com.weatherstartv

## 3. Install

After opting in, install from the Play Store:

https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.weatherstartv

It may take a few minutes after opting in for the app to become available.
